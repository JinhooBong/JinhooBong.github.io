Here's a time line of Stephen Curry's life

  • 1988 - Born in Arkon, Ohio.
  • 2005 - Recruited to Davidson college by Coach Bob McKillop.
  • 2009 - He was drafted to the Golden State Warriors as 7th overall pick and hit a record 166 three pointers as a rookie..
  • 2012-2013 - He hit 272 three pointers to set an NBA record.
  • 2014 - Made his first All Star appearance, later made first All-NBA team and became leading three point scorer for the Warriors surpassing Jason Richardson.
  • 2014-2015 - Broke his own league record for max three-pointers made, making 286.
  • 2015 - Led the Warriors to their first NBA championship since 1975 and was named the league's MVP.
  • 2015-2016 - He helped the Warriors win 24 consecutively which led to an all time record 73 wins in a season.
  • 2015-2016 - He again broke the NBA record for most threes in a season by splashing in 402. He also became the first player in the NBA history to be unanimously voted the MVP.
  • 2017 - He led the team to clinch an NBA championship by February 25 becoming the NBA's fastest championship victory.
